The Path to 65kg

Your Daily Fuel Blueprint

Target: ~2200 Calories | 100g+ Protein

7:00 AM 🌅

Wake Up Call

What to Eat 🍲

  • 💧 1-2 Glasses of Warm Water
  • 🥜 Handful of Soaked Almonds & Walnuts

Purpose & Goal ✨

Hydrate & Activate: Kickstarts your metabolism for the day.

8:30 AM ☀️

Foundation Fuel

What to Eat 🍲

  • 🥣 Oats with Milk, Banana, & Peanut Butter
  • or
  • 🍳 Tofu Bhurji & Whole Wheat Bread

Purpose & Goal ✨

Power Up: Complex carbs & protein for sustained energy.

11:30 AM 💻

Smart Snack

What to Eat 🍲

  • 🍎 An Apple or a bowl of Papaya
  • 🥛 Glass of Buttermilk (Chaas)

Purpose & Goal ✨

Stay Sharp: Keeps hunger away and your mind focused.

1:30 PM 🏙️

Midday Refuel

What to Eat 🍲

  • 🍛 Rajma/Chole + 🍚 Brown Rice + 🥕 Veg Sabzi + 🥗 Salad + 🥄 Curd (Dahi)

Purpose & Goal ✨

Build & Replenish: Your main source of nutrients for the day.

5:00 PM 🌇

Workout Power

What to Eat 🍲

  • 🍌🍌 2 Bananas

Purpose & Goal ✨

Ignite: Quick, digestible energy for your workout.

Don't Forget Your Power-Ups!

💧

Hydration is Key

Sip water all day long. Aim for 3-4 Liters. Keep a bottle with you at your desk!

🎯

Consistency > Perfection

It's okay to have an off day. The goal is to follow this 80-90% of the time. Just get back on track with the next meal!

🧂

Cook Smart

Use minimal oil (opt for ghee or mustard oil) and control your salt intake. Grill, roast, or steam when possible.
